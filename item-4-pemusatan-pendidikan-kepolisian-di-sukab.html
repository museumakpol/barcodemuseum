Pemusatan Pendidikan Kepolisian di Sukabumi / Police Education Center in Sukabumi

Deskripsi
UUD RIS menetapkan wewenang Kepolisian RIS, salah satunya adalah menyelenggarakan Pendidikan bagi pegawai polisi tingkat atas, yang merupakan pendidikan gabungan antara pendidikan kepolisian Belanda dan Republik Indonesia (RI).
R. Soebarkah yang merupakan tokoh perintis pendidikan kepolisian ditunjuk menjadi Direktur dari sekolah itu. Selain sebagai Direktur SPN, beliau juga bertugas di bagian Inspeksi Pendidikan yang pada masa itu tetap bertugas di Sukabumi.
Sebelum dibentuk Negara Kesatuan Republik Indonesia pada 17 Agustus 1950, pada 7 Juni 1950 dengan Tap Presiden RIS No. 150, organisasi-organisasi kepolisian negara-negara bagian disatukan dalam Jawatan Kepolisian Indonesia. Dalam peleburan tersebut disadari adanya kepolisian negara yang dipimpin secara sentral, baik di bidang kebijaksanaan siasat kepolisian maupun administratif dan organisatoris.

The RIS Constitution stipulated the authority of the RIS Police, one of which was to provide education for top level police officers, which was a combined education between Dutch and Republic of Indonesia (RI) police education.
R. Soebarkah, who was a pioneer in police education, was appointed as the Director of the school. Apart from being the Director of SPN, he also served in the Education Inspection section which at that time was still on duty in Sukabumi.
Before the formation of the Unitary State of the Republic of Indonesia on August 17, 1950, on June 7, 1950 with the Presidential Decree of RIS No. 150, the state police organizations were united in the Indonesian Police Bureau. In the merger, it was acknowledged that the state police were centrally led, both in the field of police tactics as well as administrative and organizational policies.
