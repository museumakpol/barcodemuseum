Kronologi Kejadian Gugurnya Ipua Adi Sarata di Tsunami Aceh, 2004 / The Death of Second Inspector Adi Sanata in the 2004 Aceh Tsunami

Deskripsi
16 Desember 2004
Ipda Adi Sanata menamatkan pendidikan di Akademi Kepolisian dan ditempatkan di Polda Lampung (yang bersangkutan belum menghadap).
Sebelum menghadap ke Polda Lampung, ia melaksanakan cuti ke tempat tinggalnya di Provinsi Aceh sampai dengan tanggal 4 Januari 2005.

26 Desember 2004
Saat terjadi gempa bumi, Ipda Adi Sanata membantu masyarakat sekitar tempat tinggalnya untuk melaksanakan tugas kemanusiaan perdana selaku perwira lulusan Akademi Kepolisian.
Ipda Adi Sanata tanpa memikirkan keselamatan dirinya memberikan pertolongan dari ancaman reruntuhan bangunan akibat gempa yang dahsyat. la mengambil kendali dan memimpin warga untuk menghindar dari kemungkinan cedera yang fatal.
Masyarakat sekitar dikumpulkan dan di perintahkan untuk menuju lapangan yang ada di sekitar. Warga yang belum mengetahui adanya bencana dijemput dari rumah untuk diselamatkan.
Setelah terjadi gempa Ipda Adi Sanata berlari untuk melakukan pengecekan terhadap hal-hal yang tidak diinginkan akibat gempa tersebut.
Ipda Adi Sanata kembali ke rumah dan mengganti pakaian dengan seragam selam dan helm arung jeram termasuk beberapa perlengkapan lainnya. Setelah itu Ipda Adi Sanata menggunakan sepeda motor untuk melihat keadaan di pinggir laut.
Di tepian pantai, Ipda Adi Sanata melihat gelombang air laut yang besar menuju ke arah pesisir.
Berbekal keyakinan dan kemampuannya. Ipda Adi Sanata kembali ke lapangan dimana semua warga dikumpulkan dan menyerukan agar semua warga untuk segera mencari tempat yang lebih tinggi.
Ipda Adi Sanata menggendong anak-anak untuk dibawa ke tempat yang dianggap aman dari terpaan air tanpa menghiraukan nasib keluarganya sendiri.
Saat terjadi gelombang tsunami lpda Adi Sanata dihantam oleh gelombang dan masih sempat membantu menaikkan anak-anak ke atas perahu serta mengulurkan tali kepada warga yang hanyut. la menempatkan batangan kayu besar yang hanyut untuk dijadikan pelampung bagi warga di tengah gelombang air.
Ipda Adi Sanata dinyatakan hilang dan akhirnya ditemukan pada tanggal 2 Januari 2005 dalam keadaan sudah tidak bernyawa dengan kondisi luka robek pada bagian kepala sebelah kanan, di lokasi tidak jauh dari tempat kediaman.

3 Januari 2005
Ipda Adi Sanata baru dapat dievakuasi karena sulitnya medan. Jenazahnya dikebumikan di tanah kelahirannya di Sabang Provinsi Aceh.


December 16, 2004
Second Inspector Adi Sanata finished his education at the Police Academy and got his placement in the Regional Police of Lampung (to which he had not got the chance to report). Before he went to Lampung, he took a work leave until January 4th 2005 to visit his home in Aceh.

December 26, 2004
When the earthquake happened, Adi Sanata helped the victims in his neighborhoods who were screaming for help in order to perform his first humanitarian task as a Police Academy graduate.
Not thinking about his own safety, Adi Sanata helped the victims who were trapped under a collapsed building. He also commanded the people to avoid the buildings, so they will not be hurt.
People were gathered around and told to go to a field nearby. Those who were still at home were being picked up.
After the earthquake, Adi Sanata checked the safety of his neighborhood
And then he returned to his home and replaced his clothes with a scuba uniform and rafting helmet and equipped himself with some tools. After that, Adi Sanata rode his motorcycle to the beach.
Arriving at the beach he noticed that a big wave was coming.
With all his faith and ability, he went back to the field and asked the people to look fo and go to a higher place for safety.
Adi Sanata carried small children to safety, without even caring for his own family.
When the tsunami happened, Adi Sanata, who was wearing a scuba uniform was hit by waves. Even then he was still trying to help some children to jump on a rescue boat and used rope and sticks to help the others.
Adi Sanata was proclaimed missing. His body was found on January 2nd 2005, near his house with an injury on the right side of his head.

January 3, 2005
His body was evacuated and buried in his hometown, Sabang, Aceh Province.
