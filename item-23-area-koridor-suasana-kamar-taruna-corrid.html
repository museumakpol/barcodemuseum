Area Koridor & Suasana Kamar Taruna / Corridor Area & Cadet’s Room

Deskripsi
Setiap dua bulan sekali dilaksanakan lomba kebersihan antar peleton. Peleton yang paling kotor diganjar membawa bendera tengkorak dalam barisan selama beberapa minggu ke depan.

Every two months a cleaning competition is held between platoons. The dirtiest platoon is rewarded carrying the skull flag in line for a couple of weeks.
