Lemari PUD (Peraturan Urusan Dalam) / PUD (Internal Affair Regulation Cupboard)

Deskripsi
Peraturan Urusan Dalam di kehidupan Taruna adalah suatu aturan yang telah ditentukan dengan prosedur tetap.
Penilaian PUD meliputi kerapihan dan kebersihan dan terdapat kategori penilaian dalam aspek lemari, meja belajar dan kasur.
Penataan PUD taruna terutama penataan lemari, meja belajar dan kasur berlangsung setiap hari, sebagai cara penilaian dalam memberikan nilai NSP kepada Taruna.
Dalam penilaian tersebut, setiap pengasuh memiliki kriteria masing-masing. Penataan PUD lemari Taruna berisi baju lipat, celana lipat, baju gantung dan celana gantung. Penataan PUD tersebut diatur oleh Batalyon wajib rapi dan bersih sehingga ada suatu peraturan yang mengikat dalam bentuk penataan PUD lemari.


Internal Affairs Regulations (Peraturan Urusan Dalam / PUD) in the life of cadets are rules that have been determined with fixed procedures.
The PUD assessment includes tidiness and cleanliness and there are assessment categories in the aspects of cupboards, study tables and mattresses.
The cadets' PUD arrangement, especially the arrangement of cupboards, study tables and mattresses, takes place every day, as a way of assessing in giving NSP scores to cadets.
In this assessment, each caregiver has their own criteria. The PUD arrangement of the cadets wardrobe contains folded clothes, folded pants, hanging clothes and hanging pants. The PUD arrangement is regulated by the Battalion which must be neat and clean so that there is a binding regulation in the form of PUD cupboard arrangement.
