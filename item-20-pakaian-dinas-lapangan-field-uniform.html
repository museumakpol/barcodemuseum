SERAGAM

Pakaian Dinas Lapangan / Field Uniform

Deskripsi
Seragam keseharian taruna, khususnya digunakan pada saat kegiatan-kegiatan yang bersifat lapangan, seperti latihan maupun kelas praktek.

The daily uniform of the cadets and especially used during field activities, such as training or practical classes.
