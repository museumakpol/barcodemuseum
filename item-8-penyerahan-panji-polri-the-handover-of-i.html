Penyerahan Panji Polri / The Handover of Indonesian National Police Flag

Deskripsi
Panji Polri diserahkan secara simbolis oleh Presiden RI pertama Ir. Soekarno kepada Kepala Kepolisian Negara Jenderal Polisi (Purn) Raden Said Soekanto Tjokrodiatmodjo pada 1 Juli 1955. Momen ini bertepatan dengan Hari Bhayangkara dan peresmian gedung Markas Besar Angkatan Kepolisian / MABAK (sekarang Mabes Polri) yang diselenggarakan di Lapangan Banteng, Jakarta Pusat.
Dalam upaya membangun kepolisian nasional, Kepala Kepolisian Negara Jenderal Polisi Raden Said Soekanto Tjokrodiatmodjo memprioritaskan pembangunan sumber daya manusia dengan membuka pendidikan untuk calon perwira polisi di Mertoyudan yang kelak melahirkan pendidikan kepolisian tingkat akademi.

The Indonesian National Police Flag was symbolically handed over by the first President of the Indonesian Republic Ir. Soekarno to the Chief of Indonesian National Police, Police General Raden Said Soekanto Tjokrodiatmodjo on July 1, 1955. The moment coincided with Bhayangkara Day and the inauguration of the Police Force Headquarters (now known as Mabes Polri) which was held in Banteng Square, Central Jakarta.
In the effort to develop the national police force, the Chief of the Indonesian National Police, Police General Raden Said Soekanto Tjokrodiatmodjo prioritized human resource development by opening education for aspiring police officers in Mertoyudan which later initiated for the police academy-level education.
