Gerilya Melibatkan Taruna / Cadets involved in Guerrilla

Deskripsi
Polisi Akademi di Mertoyudan membuka dua angkatan, Parikesit dan Abimanyu yang sering dilibatkan dalam perjuangan mempertahankan kemerdekaan.
Ketika terjadi Agresi Belanda Kedua, para instruktur dan siswa taruna turut berjuang melawan pasukan Belanda.

The Police Academy in Mertoyudan opened two batches, Parikesit and Abhimanyu, who were often involved in the struggle for independence.
When the Second Dutch Aggression occurred, the instructors and cadets also fought against the Dutch troops.
