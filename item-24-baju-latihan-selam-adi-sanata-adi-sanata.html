Baju Latihan Selam Adi Sanata / Adi Sanata’s Scuba Training Suit

Deskripsi
Baju Latihan Selam Taruna Akademi Kepolisian yang digunakan oleh mendiang Ipda Adi Sanata Putra ketika menyelamatkan warga saat terjadi gelombang tsunami.

The Scuba Training Suit of the Police Academy cadets used by the late Ipda Adi Sanata Putra when rescuing the community during the 2004 Aceh Tsunami.
